Investment & Returns Model

Discover how you can invest in AVS Dairy and the returns you can expect

To join AVS Dairy, a one-time membership fee of ₹1,200 is required, which can be used to purchase products or dine at their restaurants. After joining, members receive a unique ID and password.

The 48-Month Agreement

For an investment of ₹1,00,000, the company offers a 48-month agreement, providing a 3% monthly return. This means an investor receives ₹3,000 every month for four years, totaling ₹1,44,000 in returns. At the end of the agreement, the original investment of ₹1,00,000 is also returned.

How the 3% Return is Generated

The company uses the ₹1,00,000 investment to purchase a buffalo and a calf. The buffalo produces approximately 10 liters of milk daily. The member has two options:

Receive 1.5 liters of milk daily

Fresh milk delivered to your doorstep daily

Receive ₹3,000 per month

Equivalent to the value of the milk (1.5 liters x 30 days)

The remaining 8.5 liters of milk is sold or used in the company's restaurants, which is how the company generates its profit.

Withdrawal Policy

Understand the terms for withdrawing your investment

Full Withdrawal
